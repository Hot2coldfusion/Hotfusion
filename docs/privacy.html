Privacy Policy – Frau Zahnspange Publisher

Effective date: May 31, 2026 · Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Who we are

This application ("Frau Zahnspange Publisher", the "App") is a private, single-user creator tool operated by the account owner of @frau_zahnspange:

Frau Zahnspange Publisher
Email: fzakfo2015@gmail.com

The App is used exclusively to manage the TikTok account @frau_zahnspange, which is operated by the account owner. There are no other end users.

2. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data the App processes, where it is stored, and what choices the account owner has. Because the App has exactly one user (the account owner herself), there are no third-party end users whose personal data is processed.

3. Data we process

  • TikTok access tokens – from TikTok OAuth, stored encrypted locally, kept until revoke/expiry
  • Post history (own posts only) – from TikTok Content Posting API, local JSON, owner-controlled retention
  • Comment cache (own posts only) – from TikTok Comments API, local JSON, rolling
  • Owner's command history – Telegram chat between owner and her own assistant bot, local logs

4. Data we do NOT collect

  • Personal data of TikTok followers or any third parties beyond the public comment text on the owner's own posts
  • Direct messages
  • Insights about non-owner accounts
  • Location data
  • Analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers

Comment text fetched from the owner's own posts is held only in a local cache long enough for the owner to read and reply on TikTok directly. It is not transmitted to any third-party service.

5. How data is used

  • Schedule and publish posts to @frau_zahnspange
  • Notify the owner about new comments on her own posts so she can reply on TikTok
  • Maintain a local log of what the App did on her behalf

No automated profiling, no advertising use, no sharing with third parties.

6. Third-party services

The App communicates exclusively with the TikTok APIs (open.tiktokapis.com). No other third-party services receive data from this App.

7. Legal basis (GDPR)

  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR – consent given by the account owner via OAuth
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR – legitimate interest of the account owner in managing her own social media presence efficiently

8. Data subject rights

The owner may at any time:

  • Revoke the OAuth grant in TikTok account settings, which immediately terminates the App's access
  • Delete any local data on her own machine
  • Contact fzakfo2015@gmail.com with any question

9. Data security

  • Access tokens are stored encrypted at rest on the owner's local machine
  • The App does not run on a public server; there is no public endpoint
  • Communication with TikTok uses HTTPS

10. Children

The App is not directed at, and does not knowingly process data of, children under 16.

11. Changes to this policy

The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any change.
